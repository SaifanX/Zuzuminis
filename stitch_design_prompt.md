# STITCH DESIGN PROMPT: ZUZU MINIS

## 1. PROJECT PERSONA & CORE MISSION
**Project Name**: Zuzu Minis
**Brand Identity**: A "Playful Premium" kidswear boutique based in Bangalore, India.
**Mission**: Transform a high-end physical boutique experience into an elite, cinematic digital storefront.
**Target Audience**: Discerning parents in Jayanagar/JP Nagar/South Bangalore who value skin-friendly, organic fabrics and minimalist luxury.
**Aesthetic Anchor**: "Quiet Luxury" for children. Think *Vogue Kids* editorial meets Apple’s spatial design.

## 2. VISUAL IDENTITY SYSTEM (THE SPEC)
**Color Palette**:
- `Base`: #FDFCF9 (Warm Ivory / Sand) - Must feel creamy and expensive.
- `Zuzu Blue`: #4A90E2 (Muted Teal / Soft Blue) - For "zuzu" branding and high-energy accents.
- `Minis Pink`: #FFB6C1 (Soft Peach / Pink) - For "minis" branding and secondary warmth.
- `Contrast`: #1A1A1A (Deep Charcoal) - For elite typography and navigation.
- `Highlight`: #F5E6BE (Butter Yellow) - For subtle hovers and secondary cards.

**Typography Strategy**:
- `Headings`: **Searghy** (Display Serif). Bold, playful, and high-character. Use for massive, statement headlines.
- `Body/Accents`: **Kiddos** (Rounded/Friendly). Use for secondary copy, badges, and labels to maintain a kid-like warmth.
- `Branding`: The logo is a **Circular Badge** featuring a minimalist baby face with a single curl.

## 3. UI ARCHITECTURE (AIDA PRINCIPLES)
**Chapter 01: The Statement (Attention)**
- **Direction**: We're looking for a hero experience that feels immediate and bold. Massive asymmetrical H1s and an interactive element like a glass mascot could be a great way to start the story.

**Chapter 02: The Collection (Interest)**
- **Direction**: Explore cinematic ways to browse the products. Whether it's a horizontal scroll or something entirely new, the goal is to make the fabric quality the star.

**Chapter 03: The Narrative (Desire)**
- **Direction**: We want to weave in the Bangalore studio story. Stacking cards or other interactive narrative elements would help build trust in our craft.

**Chapter 04: The Shop (Action)**
- **Direction**: Keep the shopping experience clean and efficient. Minimalist cards with helpful, subtle interactions would fit the boutique vibe perfectly.

## 4. DESIGN "SPELLS" & TECHNICAL CUES
- **Weightlessness**: Every element should feel buoyant. Use GSAP for floating reveals.
- **Spatial Depth**: Implement glassmorphism (Transmission/Refraction) for UI cards.
- **Grain Overlay**: Apply a subtle 3% grain texture globally for a cinematic film look.
- **No Placeholders**: Every image must feel like professional photography from a high-end shoot.

## 5. DESIGNER INSTRUCTION
"Design a web experience that feels like a physical boutique in Jayanagar. It must be elite but warm. Use massive typography as a structural element. Avoid all generic ecommerce templates. The site should feel like a 'Chapter-based' story rather than a product list. Focus on the relationship between the baby face mascot and the high-end fabrics."
